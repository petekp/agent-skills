# LLM Word and Phrase List

Sourced from [Wikipedia: Signs of AI writing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Signs_of_AI_writing) and [WikiProject AI Cleanup](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_AI_Cleanup/AI_catchphrases).

These words and phrases are not banned. They are statistical signals — individually harmless, collectively diagnostic. Flag text containing clusters of 3+ items from this list for rewriting.

### Tailing Participle Phrases

Attaching superficial significance analysis via -ing clauses at the end of sentences:

- emphasizing the significance (of)
- reflecting the continued relevance (of)
- highlighting the need (for)
- underscoring the importance (of)
- marking a significant milestone (in)
- demonstrating a commitment (to)
- cementing their legacy
- showcasing their versatility

### Copula Avoidance

LLMs substitute elaborate verbs for simple "is/are/has":

| LLM Writes | Human Writes |
|---|---|
| serves as | is |
| stands as | is |
| functions as | is |
| features | has |
| offers | has |
| boasts | has |
| ventured into politics as a candidate | was a candidate |
| mark the beginning of | began |

### Chatbot Leakage

Obvious copy-paste artifacts from chatbot conversations:

- I hope this helps
- Of course! / Certainly!
- Would you like me to...
- Is there anything else...
- Let me know if...
- As an AI language model
- As of my last knowledge update
- Here's a more detailed breakdown

## Section Heading Patterns

- Title Case On Every Word (AI default; humans use sentence case)
- "X and Y" conjunction headings: "Early Life and Career", "Content and Features", "Awards and Recognition"
- Preference for "Recognition" over "Reception"

## Markdown / Formatting Artifacts

- Excessive **boldface** for emphasis in mechanical patterns
- Unfilled template blanks: [insert name], [your topic here]
- Markdown syntax in non-markdown contexts (# headers, **bold**, - bullets)
- Emoji as section markers
